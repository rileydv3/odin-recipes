Vegan Pasta

Description:

This vegan pasta is made with plant-based crumbles, peppers, onions, protein-packed noodles, meat-free spaghetti sauce, and Italian seasoning.

Ingredients:

  • Green and Red Pepper
  • Yellow Onion
  • Plant-based Crumbles
  • Protein-plus Penne Pasta
  • Your Favorite Spaghetti Sauce
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps:

  1. Cut up peppers/onions, and saute them for 5 minutes using the saute setting on the Instant Pot.
  2. Add garlic, plant-based crumbles, and seasoning to pot, and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Press Cancel on Instant Pot. Add 4 cups water, pasta, and sauce to pot. Set timer to 1 minute on high pressure.
  4. After timer reads 15 minutes, release pressure from Instant Pot. Enjoy!
